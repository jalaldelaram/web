Course: Facility Layout

Course Overview

Facility Layout is a vital course in Industrial Engineering that focuses on the design and arrangement of physical resources to optimize manufacturing efficiency and minimize costs. The course covers various layout planning techniques and strategies used in production systems, warehouses, and service facilities.

Syllabus

Course Duration: 14 weeks

Course Prerequisites: Basic understanding of industrial engineering concepts and process analysis

Course Objectives: - Learn the principles of facility design and layout planning - Understand the relationship between production processes and layout decisions - Explore different types of facility layouts (e.g., process layout, product layout, fixed-position layout) - Analyze the impact of layout decisions on operational efficiency and cost

Assessment Methods: - Homework and assignments: 30% - Midterm exam: 30% - Final exam: 40%
