Welcome!

Dr. Jalal Delaram is an Assistant Professor of Industrial Engineering at the University of Tehran, College of Engineering. His is expertise lies in the application of Game Theory, Mechanism Design, Operations Research, Statistical and Time Series Analysis to solve challenges in Supply Chain Management, Market Design, Humanitarian Aid Systems, Economics, and Operations Management.
